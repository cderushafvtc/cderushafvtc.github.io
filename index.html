Introduction

Hi, I'm Chelsea! I am a Web Development student with a passion for both creative technology and problem-solving.

I'm currently pursuing my degree in Web Development after taking a three-year break from my studies. During that time, my family and I relocated from Wisconsin to Michigan, where I had the opportunity to work as a media production coordinator. In this role, I oversaw a team of talented 3D artists who used industry-standard tools like Unreal Engine, Maya, Blender, and ZBrush to bring complex projects to life.

It was an incredible experience working alongside artists who had contributed to major films like Harry Potter, Lord of the Rings, Into the Spider-Verse, and The Polar Express, and learning from a director with credits on The Matrix and Field of Dreams. This experience deepened my appreciation for the intersection of technology, creativity, and storytelling.

When I'm not coding or studying, you'll find me tinkering with personal projects, whether that's 3D printing, finding creative ways to use Raspberry Pis, or automating my home. I also love spending time outdoors with my husband and kids, trail riding and tending to my flower garden. And of course, my two Labrador Retrievers, Maize and Lou, are always nearby keeping me company.

I'm excited to be back in school, refreshing my skills, and diving deeper into web development. I'm particularly interested in exploring how my background in media production and my technical skills can come together to create engaging, user-centered digital experiences.
